Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your device Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > ChimneyCheck > Cancel Subscription.

Does ChimneyCheck work offline?

Yes. Complete full inspections with no cell service. Photos, checklists, and reports all work offline. Data syncs when you reconnect.

What NFPA 211 levels are supported?

ChimneyCheck includes complete checklists for Level I and Level II inspections per NFPA 211 standards.

How do I delete my account?

Open ChimneyCheck > Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

Can I add my company logo to reports?

Yes. Go to Settings > Company Profile to add your logo, company name, license number, and contact information. All PDF reports will include your branding.
