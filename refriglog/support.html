Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your device Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > RefrigLog > Cancel Subscription.

Does RefrigLog work offline?

Yes. All features work without an internet connection. Data syncs automatically when you reconnect.

How do I delete my account?

Open RefrigLog > Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Data is encrypted on your device and during transmission. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What EPA regulations does RefrigLog help with?

RefrigLog helps you document compliance with EPA Clean Air Act Section 608 and the AIM Act, including refrigerant charge logging, leak rate tracking, and audit report generation. The app is a record-keeping tool, not legal advice.
