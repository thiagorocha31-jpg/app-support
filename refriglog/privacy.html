RefrigLog - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

Parallax Value Partners LLC ("we", "us") operates the RefrigLog mobile application. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and name via our authentication provider (Clerk).

Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics data (app opens, feature usage) via PostHog to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included.

Refrigerant Logs: Equipment records, refrigerant charge data, cylinder inventory, and inspection reports you create are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. If you enable cloud sync, this data is stored on secure servers.

Crash Reports: We collect anonymized crash reports via Sentry to fix bugs and improve stability.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, or other apps
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the RefrigLog service
  • To process your subscription via Apple's App Store (we do not see your payment details)
  • To send important service updates (you can opt out)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage & Security

Your refrigerant logs are stored locally on your device by default. Data is encrypted at rest. If cloud sync is enabled, data is transmitted via TLS and stored on encrypted servers in the United States.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings > Delete Account. Local data is removed immediately. Cloud data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

  • Sentry (crash reporting): Privacy Policy
  • PostHog (analytics): Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

RefrigLog is not intended for use by anyone under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
