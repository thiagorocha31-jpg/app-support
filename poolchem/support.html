Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your device Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > PoolChem > Cancel Subscription.

Does PoolChem work offline?

Yes. All features work without an internet connection. Data syncs automatically when you reconnect.

How accurate are the dosing calculations?

Dosing calculations are based on manufacturer-published formulas for 47 real chemical products. Always test water after treatment to verify results.

How do I delete my account?

Open PoolChem > Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

Can I manage more than 5 pools on the free plan?

The free plan supports up to 5 pool profiles. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited pools at $12.99/month flat (no per-pool fees).
